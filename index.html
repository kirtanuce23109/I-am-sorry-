What I did, and what I want you to feel now

What I did

I didn't treat you the way you deserve, Mam. Not with cruelty, but with carelessness — moments where I should have been gentler, more attentive, more present, and I wasn't. I let you feel unimportant when you are the opposite of that. That is on me, fully, and I'm not going to explain it away.

What I want you to feel now

Safe. Comfortable. Heard. I want you to know that I see how I've fallen short, that I'm not asking you to just move past it, and that you never have to shrink yourself or settle for less care than you deserve — not from me, not from anyone.
